Lil Stuart was a tiny man, to be exact, he was three foot eight but he lived a quiet life. He had a big poofy afro and was built like a buff meatball. He enjoyed his life in his small town that was located in the West named Little Creek.

But the peace didn’t last for long when Texas Red of the Yee Yee nation attacked the Little Creek in 1860. Texas Red pillaged his small village of their resources and enslaved Lil Stuart’s friends and family. During the attack Lil Stuart was hiding in his underground lair. After the main attack was over Texas Red stationed a handful of his men at the village.

Lil Stuart made up his mind and equipped a number of items before setting off to take down the evil doer Texas Red. He travelled all across the West facing many obstacles until he reached Texas Red’s base and defeated Texas Red and the Yee Yee nation. He set free all of his friends and family and acquired all of the Yee Yee nation's wealth and treasure.
