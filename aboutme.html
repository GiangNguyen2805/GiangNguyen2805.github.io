I think the first step of being a successful engineer is to have a systematic and efficient learning. It is very important for an engineer to understand the fundamentals and basic principles of engineering. By memorizing and copying out things might help me to remember notes, but I know that these only just work for a short period. So I need to set out to understand the meaning of what I have to learn instead of concentrate my time repeating things, which are surface approach to be an engineer. Moreover, after I finishing my 4 years of university, I want to learn as much from others' experience. I believe that even though, books are one of the source that could be trusted the most but actually, not everything is in the books. So learning from others that have long time experiences are one of the master key tools that I want to have. Later on, I want my colleagues, my manager or even my boss to see my potential and that everyday I could walk into the office with a happy mind.
